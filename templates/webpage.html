Trending @HELP

Trending @HELP is a live-streaming website that helps first responders prioritize recent distress tweets after a disaster from most to least panicked using Microsoft's Cognitive Services Text Analytics API.

Trending @HELP was inspired by reading a Texas Monthly article about first responders' use of social media during Hurricane Harvey First responders during Harvey received an overwhelming amount of social media messages and had to manually determine which ones could save lives. Our website helps automate that process, acting like an "Emergency Room" for Tweets.
